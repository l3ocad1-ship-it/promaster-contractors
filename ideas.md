## CHOSEN APPROACH: Response 3 — Bold Contractor Brand / High Contrast Sales Machine

This approach best serves the business goal: a page that **sells**. Every section has a clear conversion objective. The dark/light alternation creates visual rhythm without complexity. The orange CTA system creates a clear visual hierarchy that guides the user toward calling or submitting a form. The Montserrat type system is clean, modern and highly legible on mobile.
